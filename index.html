BoilDB is a proposed weekly research seminar at Purdue University aimed at fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing among graduate students and researchers. The seminar will serve as a platform for students to present their ongoing work, exchange feedback, and engage in interdisciplinary discussions. While the core focus will be on database research, we aim to include students from related fields such as computer architecture and systems to encourage cross-domain insights and potential collaborations.
